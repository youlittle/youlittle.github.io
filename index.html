Tony Stark

Tony Stark is a smart guy and the owner of the iron man armor.

Tony Stark is the owner of Stark Indestries, a company that has helped many people in the past. His armor sustains his life, he would not have survived the plane crash he went through without the armor>

Tony lives in a mansion on a hill in Malibu CA. he is known for his brilant inventions and Iron man armor. He created a laser targeting system that was for mining, but Obedia Stane turned it into a walking weapon.
